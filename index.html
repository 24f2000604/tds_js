Risk Assessment Model Snippet (Python)

An excerpt from our Python script demonstrating the calculation of Historic Value at Risk (VaR). 

            
import numpy as np
import pandas as pd

def calculate_var(returns, confidence_level=0.95):
    """Calculates the Historic Value at Risk (VaR)"""
    sorted_returns = np.sort(returns)
    var_index = int(len(sorted_returns) * (1 - confidence_level))
    return sorted_returns[var_index]

Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM)

We use the CAPM formula to estimate the expected return of an asset, considering its systematic risk (β).

$$ E(R_i) = R_f + \beta_i \cdot (E(R_m) - R_f) $$

Where: $R_f$ is the risk-free rate, $\beta_i$ is the asset's beta, and $E(R_m) - R_f$ is the market risk premium.

This fundamental equation ensures our investments meet minimum return requirements based on the market risk they carry. This is how we justify capital allocation.
